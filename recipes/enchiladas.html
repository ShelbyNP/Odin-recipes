Enchiladas

Level: Beginner
Time estimated:60 minutes
Recipe Source

Ingredients needed:

  • 2 tablespoons avocado oil (or olive oil)
  • 1 small white onion, peeled and diced
  • 1 1/2 lbs of bonless, skinlless chicken brests, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
  • sea salt and black pepper
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 3 cups mexican-blend shredded cheese
  • 1 batch of red enchilada sauce, or 1 (14 ounce) can of enchilada red sauce
  • optional toppings (cilantro, red onions, avocado, sour cream, etc.

Instructions:

  1. Prep oven and enchilada sauce. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Saute the filling mixture. Once onion, dice chicken, chilies, and beans are cooked, removed from heat and set aside.
  3. Heat up the tortilla for about 10 seconds in the microwave to make them easier to work with.
  4. Assemble the enchiladas, adding spponfuls of chicken and cheese, add remaining sauce to top of enchiladas.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes uncovered, until the tortillas are slightly crispy on the outside.
