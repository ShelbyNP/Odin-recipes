Asian Chicken Wraps

Level: beginner
Time estimated:15 minutes
Recipe Source

Ingredients needed:

  • 3 cups coleslaw mix
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/3 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cooked chicken breasts, chopped or sliced
  • 6 large tortillas
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 3 tablespoons unseasoned rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the peanu sauce ingredients.
  2. In another bowl, add cabbag, carrots, cilantro, and peanuts. Pour the sauce over the mixture and stir. Let this sit while you prepare the tortilla and chicken.
  3. Heat up the tortilla for about 10 seconds in the microwave to make them easier to work with.
  4. Divide the chicke and coleslaw between each tortilla
  5. Roll tightly and secure with toothpicks if necessary.
